### Atomic Notation 


$$
\ce{^{17}_{8}O^{2-}}
$$

* 2- is **charge** = protons - electrons
* 17 is **mass number** = protons + neutrons 
* 8 is the **atomic number** = protons 
* O is the **element symbol** goes hand in hand with the **atomic number**
